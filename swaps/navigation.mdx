description: 'How are fees taken from the service?'
icon: 'map'
---

Trade fees no trading fees are taken on the platform. Instead, batch auction swaps take a percentage of the surplus as a keeper fee while the rest is pocketed as protocol revenue. 

Using instant swap, there is no fee outside of referrals rebates. While batch auction orders have the ability to capture fees for the protocol based on the positive slippage collected.
